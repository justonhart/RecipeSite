## Ingredients
- 2 cups water
- 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 4 tbsp honey
- 2 tsp fresh ginger, minced
- 2 tsp fresh garlic, minced

## Preparation
1. Whisk all ingredients together over medium heat until boiling
2. Remove from heat when desired thickness is reached
